Tee Bets — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026 · Developer: Bo Nicholson

Tee Bets is a golf side-game tracker and settlement calculator. It records friendly game results and calculates who owes whom — it never holds, moves, or processes money. We designed it to keep your data on your own devices and in your own iCloud. This policy explains exactly what that means.

The short version

  • We have no servers and no backend of our own. The app runs on Apple's CloudKit using your iCloud account.
  • We don't track you. No advertising, no analytics SDKs, no data brokers, no third-party trackers.
  • Your rounds, scores, and profile live in your private iCloud, which the developer cannot read.
  • The only data shared beyond your iCloud is optional community course information (course name, location, pars, and stroke indexes) — never your scores, bets, or personal details.

What the app stores, and where

1. Your account (Sign in with Apple). When you sign in, Apple provides your name and an email address (which may be a private Apple relay address) plus a stable user identifier. These are stored in your private iCloud database and used only to set up your profile and sync it across your own devices. We, the developer, cannot access your private iCloud database.

2. Your golf data. Rounds, players, scores, settlements, your roster, and Ryder Cup tournaments are stored in your private iCloud database and synced across your devices. When you share a round with your group, that round is shared directly between your iCloud accounts via Apple's CloudKit sharing so everyone can score together; it is not routed through any server we operate.

3. The community course catalog (optional). If you choose "Save & share this course," the app publishes course data only — course name, tee, locality, geographic location, slope/rating, hole count, pars, and stroke indexes — to a shared public CloudKit catalog so other golfers can find that course. This catalog contains no scores, no bets, and no personal information. You can use the app fully without ever publishing a course.

Device permissions

  • Camera — used only to scan a paper scorecard and read its pars and stroke indexes on your device. Scorecard photos are processed locally and are not uploaded to us.
  • Location — used only on your device to help find nearby golf courses while you search. Your location is not stored and never leaves your device.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell or share your personal data.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or analytics.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites; the app shows no App Tracking Transparency prompt because there is nothing to track.
  • We do not process payments or facilitate real-money gambling. Players settle up among themselves, outside the app.

Data retention and deletion

Your golf data persists in your iCloud until you delete it. You can:

  • Delete individual rounds, cups, courses, or roster players inside the app.
  • Sign out or switch Apple ID from the profile screen.
  • Remove all app data by deleting the app and, if desired, clearing its data in iOS Settings → your name → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → Tee Bets.

To request removal of a course you published to the community catalog, contact us at the email below.

Children's privacy

Tee Bets is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the effective date above and post the revised policy at the same URL.
